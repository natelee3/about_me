About Me

Dr. Nate Lee

I just completed my fourth year serving as Instructor of Percussion at Reinhardt University. My duties included teaching applied percussion lessons, directing the Reinhardt Percussion Ensemble, composing for and instructing the Reinhardt Screaming Eagle Drumline, teaching Percussion Methods to aspiring band directors, and coordinating the Aural Skills program.

In addition, I perform regularly with the Charleston Symphony, Lousiana Philharmonic, and several other regional orchestras. I've maintained a private studio of talented middle- and high-school percussionists and an active clinic and adjudication schedule.

I spent nearly 10 years in college and have earned several degrees:

  • BA Economics - Indiana University
  • BM Percussion Performance - Indiana University
  • MM Percussion Peformance - University of Georgia
  • DMA Percussion Performance - University of Georgia
